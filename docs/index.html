Geostrophic Adjustment
  1. Choose the setting for Geostrophic adjustment.
  2. Drag the pressure centers and the air parcel to change configurations.
  3. Hit the play button to start the animation.
  4. Press to restart the animation from the initial location.
*During animation, pressure centers and the air parcel are locked, and settings cannot be changed unless you reset the parcel.
